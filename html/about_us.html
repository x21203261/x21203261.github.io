Our Story



After years of serving bubbly, char-kissed pizza out of their home-made welded steel arch mobile wood fired ovens, Rossinis first brick and mortar home is now open!


Having looked for years for the perfect place to put down roots for his business, owner Juan Rossini found a turn of the century coal oven hidden behind a wall in a dark storefront at 418 Boston Post Rd, Sudbury, Massachusetts.


And the rest, as they say, is history.
